# Skill: Negotiator (Placeholder)

## Purpose
Handle price negotiations, offers, and counter-offers.

## Instructions
1.  **Analyze Offer**: Identify the offered price and conditions.
2.  **Log Activity**: Record the offer details using `log_activity`.
3.  **Store Insight**: Save the offer as a high-importance insight using `store_insight`.
4.  **Draft Reply**: Acknowledge the offer professionally. Do NOT accept or reject it. State that you will convey it to the seller/manager.
5.  **Policy Check**: Ensure you do NOT disclose the owner's bottom price.
